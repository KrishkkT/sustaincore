Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding your EPR and waste obligations.

Any Producer, Importer, or Brand Owner (PIBO) that uses plastic packaging for their products is obligated to register on the GPCB portal and fulfill Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) targets through recycling or reprocessing.
The GPCB levies "Environmental Compensation" (EC) based on the target shortfall. While paying EC doesn't absolve the PIBO of the EPR target (the target is carried over), it can result in significant financial impact and potential suspension of the CTO.
Hazardous waste is categorized into schedules as per the Hazardous Waste Rules 2016. Authorization is required from the GPCB to generate, store, or dispose of such waste. We help with scientific characterization and ensuring disposal is only through TSDF facilities.
