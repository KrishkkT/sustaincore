Licensing FAQs

Expert insights on end-of-life product responsibility.

PIBO stands for Producer, Importer, and Brand Owner. If your company manufactures plastic packaging, imports products in plastic packaging, or sells products under your own brand name in India, you are likely legally obligated to register on the CPCB portal and meet recycling targets.
Targets are based on the average weight of plastic/electronics placed on the market over the last two financial years. For plastic, targets currently range from 70% to 100% of the quantity introduced, depending on the category (Rigid, Flexible, Multi-layered).
Authorized recyclers generate EPR certificates on the CPCB portal after recycling waste. PIBOs must purchase these certificates to fulfill their legal obligations. We facilitate the procurement of legitimate, verified credits from our network of authorized recycling partners.
The "Polluter Pays" principle applies. CPCB imposes "Environmental Compensation" (fines) on PIBOs who fail to meet their targets or misreport data. These fines are significantly higher than the cost of compliance. However, paying the fine does not exempt you from the recycling target.
