Navigation through global ESG benchmarks.

EcoVadis scores are relative to your industry peer group. Generally, a score above 50 earns a Bronze medal, while 65+ earns Silver, and 75+ earns Gold. However, global supply chains often look for continuous improvement year-on-year rather than just a single benchmark score.
The process begins with a formal commitment letter, followed by developing targets that meet the SBTi criteria. Your targets must then be submitted for an official validation by the SBTi technical team. We manage this entire technical pipeline for you.
Double Materiality looks at how ESG issues impact your company’s financial value (outside-in) and how your company impacts society and the environment (inside-out). It is now a mandatory requirement under EU CSRD and a best practice for high-quality disclosure.
