01 — Planet in distress

02 — Industry impact
37Gt
Global CO₂ emissions from industry in 2023
Industrial CO₂ hit a record high in 2023. Manufacturing, energy and construction account for over 70% of all global emissions.
Source: IEA / Global Carbon Project

Projected GDP loss from climate damage by 2050
Climate risk will wipe 10–23% from global GDP by 2100. Companies without a sustainability strategy face the steepest losses.
Source: Swiss Re / WEF

CBAM Advisory & Reporting
