Frequently Asked Questions

Technical guidance on aqueous analysis.

As per SPCB/SPCB norms, most industries need to monitor discharge parameters daily (internal) and conduct NABL-certified third-party testing monthly or quarterly, depending on their "Red/Orange" category and consent conditions.
The IS 10500:2012 standard specifies around 33 essential parameters including pH, TDS, Heavy Metals (Lead, Mercury), and microbiological counts (E.coli). Our NABL reports provide a pass/fail summary against these specific limits.
If your CTO (Consent to Operate) specifies ZLD conditions, you must maintain exhaustive water-balance charts and salt recovery logs. We provide technical auditing to verify that no untreated wastewater is leaving the perimeter.
