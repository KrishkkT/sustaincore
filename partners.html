Who We Partner With

Four kinds of partners
we're actively seeking.

We believe sustainability advances fastest when the right organisations work together. We are building a partner ecosystem that spans industry, technology, academia, and certification — united by one standard: credibility.

01
Industry & Trade Bodies
National and sectoral associations whose members face ESG compliance requirements. We can support member education, framework guidance, and policy-aligned sustainability programming.
02
Certification & Standards Agencies
Bodies that issue environmental certifications and standards. We can serve as an implementation and advisory partner, helping enterprises prepare for and achieve certification under your standards.
03
Technology & Software Platforms
ESG data tools, carbon tracking software, and environmental reporting platforms. We can co-develop workflows, refer clients, and provide the consulting layer that turns data into action.
04
Academic & Research Institutions
Universities, research centres, and think tanks working on sustainability, climate science, or environmental policy. We welcome collaboration on applied research, training programmes, and knowledge exchange.
