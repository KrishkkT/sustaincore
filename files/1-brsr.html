Why it matters

BRSR is no longer a choice.
It is a legal obligation.

Under SEBI’s mandate, BRSR has fundamentally changed India’s ESG disclosure landscape. Here is what is at stake for your business.

01
Regulatory Enforcement
SEBI has embedded BRSR compliance into listing obligations. Non-disclosure or inadequate disclosure triggers regulatory scrutiny, exchange notices and potential financial penalties for listed companies.
02
Investor Due Diligence
Institutional investors, ESG funds and foreign portfolio investors now screen BRSR quality before capital allocation. A weak disclosure signals governance risk and depresses ESG ratings.
03
Supply Chain Credibility
Global buyers — particularly from the EU, US and Japan — use BRSR data to evaluate Indian suppliers on sustainability criteria. Your report directly impacts procurement decisions.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

BRSR is currently mandatory for the top 1,000 listed companies by market capitalisation on Indian exchanges, effective FY 2022-23. BRSR Core — the assured subset of 39 KPIs — applies to the top 150 companies from FY 2023-24, expanding to all 1,000 by FY 2026-27. Even companies below these thresholds should treat voluntary BRSR disclosure as a strategic necessity, as investor and procurement expectations are rising sharply across all listed businesses.

First-time BRSR reports take 10–16 weeks from kickoff to publication. This timeline includes materiality assessment (2-3 weeks), cross-functional data collection (4-6 weeks), drafting and internal review (3-4 weeks), and assurance preparation (2-3 weeks). Annual cycles from the second year run in 6–8 weeks because baseline systems and data templates are already in place. Our pre-built data templates and phased review process prevent the last-minute bottlenecks that frequently delay first-time reporters.

BRSR Core is the 39-indicator assured subset of the full BRSR — covering the most financially material ESG KPIs. Mandatory third-party assurance for BRSR Core applies to the top 150 listed companies by market cap from FY 2023-24, expanding to the top 1,000 by FY 2026-27. If you are in the top 500 by market cap today, you should be preparing your BRSR Core assurance process now, as the assurance engagement requires your data systems and evidence documentation to be in order before the assurance provider arrives.

Yes — and we recommend it. BRSR, GRI Universal Standards 2021, TCFD and CDP Climate all share substantial data overlap across environmental performance, governance structures, climate risk and stakeholder engagement. Our integrated data architecture collects this data once in a single, internally consistent dataset and maps it to each framework simultaneously. This reduces total data collection effort by 35-40% and eliminates the inconsistencies that arise from running separate workstreams per framework.

SEBI requires limited third-party assurance of BRSR Core indicators for applicable companies. The assurance engagement verifies data accuracy, completeness and the reasonableness of methodologies used. We build a complete evidence documentation package — calculation methodologies, source data, verification chains, and assumption documentation — before your assurance provider arrives. This typically reduces assurance fees by 20-30% and turnaround time by 2-3 weeks, because auditors spend less time reconstructing how numbers were derived.
