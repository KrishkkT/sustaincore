Consent to Renew (CTR)

A GPCB Consent to Operate is not permanent — it expires and must be renewed before the validity date to avoid operating illegally. An expired CTO means your factory is running without authorisation , regardless of how many years you have been compliant. We track every consent expiry across your facilities and manage the complete renewal process — so your operations never face a compliance gap.

Why it matters

An expired consent is
the same as no consent.

Every CTO has a fixed validity period. Once it expires, the unit is operating without GPCB authorisation — regardless of its compliance track record. The consequences are immediate and severe.

Immediate Legal Exposure
Operating after consent expiry violates both the Water Act 1974 and Air Act 1981. GPCB enforcement officers may issue a show-cause notice, seal plant machinery or initiate closure proceedings — all of which create immediate production disruption and reputational damage.
Audit & Due Diligence Risk
Lenders, insurers, export buyers and ESG-rating agencies regularly verify CTO validity during compliance audits. An expired consent found during a bank renewal review, ISO audit or buyer qualification can trigger financial covenants and procurement disqualification.
Late Renewal Penalty Risk
Applying for renewal after the consent has already expired exposes the company to GPCB late fees and potentially additional scrutiny that extends the processing time. In some cases, GPCB may require a fresh application rather than a renewal — a significantly more complex process.

Consent Validity Audit & Expiry Mapping

90-Day Advance Renewal Initiation
We initiate the renewal process 90 days before each consent expiry date — ensuring that even if GPCB takes its full 120 days for processing, your consent is renewed before the existing one lapses.
Compliance Performance Review
Before filing each renewal, we review your consent condition compliance record, update monitoring data, address any previous GPCB observations and ensure the renewal application reflects actual operational parameters.
Renewal Application Preparation & Filing

Post-Renewal Conditions Update
After each renewal, we review updated consent conditions, revise the compliance register and reset the next renewal cycle trigger. Any new conditions introduced in the renewed consent are immediately tracked and actioned.
