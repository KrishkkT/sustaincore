The penalty of non-compliance

Environmental Compensation —
the cost of target shortfalls.

Non-fulfillment of EPR targets triggers "Environmental Compensation" (EC) based on the quantity of shortfall. Compensation is penal and does not absolve the liability.

01
Portal-Based Enforcement
The CPCB portal automatically calculates targets based on your sales data. Any unfulfilled balance at the end of the financial year is automatically flagged for compensation assessments.
02
Target Carry-Over
Paying Environmental Compensation does not reset your target. The shortfall is carried forward to the next year, creating a compounding liability if not managed strategically.
03
Registration Suspension
Persistent non-filing of annual returns or non-fulfillment of targets can lead to suspension of PIBO registration, effectively halting your legal right to import or produce.
